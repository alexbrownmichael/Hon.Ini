About

Comrade Ini Ememobong Essien is the Akwa Ibom State Commissioner of Information and Strategy. He hails from Use Ndon, Ibiono Ibom Local Government Area. Until his appointment as commissioner into the Akwa Ibom State Executive Council, Comrade Essien was the Publicity Secretary of the Peoples Democratic Party, PDP, Akwa Ibom State Chapter, a position he held from May 2016.
